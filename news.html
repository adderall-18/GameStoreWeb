Performance and Qulaity of Life

Microsoft's new update aims to increase the load times on NVMe SSD's . This feature in particular is the most exciting if you are a gamer. This not only means faster load times but also means decreased texture loading times thus giving us a seamless, smooth experience while gaming.

The minimum system requirements to run Windows 11 are:
